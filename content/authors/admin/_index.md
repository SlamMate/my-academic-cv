I am a PhD candidate in the Computer Vision Group at the University of Amsterdam. My research focuses on neural visual SLAM under the supervision of [Dr. Martin R. Oswald](https://scholar.google.de/citations?user=biytQP8AAAAJ&hl=en), [Dr. Sezer Karaoglu](https://karaoglusezer.github.io/), and [Prof. Theo Gevers](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=yqsvxQgAAAAJ&hl=en). I also collaborate with [Prof. Arnoud Visser](https://staff.fnwi.uva.nl/a.visser/) on real-world data collection and robotics algorithms.

During my MSc studies at the University of Glasgow, I worked on camera-assisted autonomous exploration for visually impaired users under the supervision of [Dr. Jianglin Lan](https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/engineering/staff/jianglinlan/).

Outside research, I enjoy beatboxing and long-distance running (10 km in 40 minutes).
